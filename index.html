About Me

Results-driven Merchandiser with more than four years of experience in outerwear order planning, capacity analysis, buyer management, and supply chain coordination for international brands including Decathlon and Li & Fung.

Experienced in Time & Action (T&A) management, stock optimization, lead-time negotiation, and cross-functional collaboration. I combine hands-on industry experience with formal business education through my PGD in Garments Business from IBA, University of Dhaka.

My goal is to build efficient supply chains, improve business performance, and create value for global apparel brands through strategic merchandising and data-driven decision making.
